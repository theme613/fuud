Frequently Asked Questions

How do I contact a seller?
You can contact a seller by clicking the "Chat with Seller" button on any listing page. You can also call the seller directly if they have provided their phone number. Always use the in-app chat for your safety.
How do I report a suspicious ad?
Click the "Report" button on the listing page and select the appropriate reason. Our team will review the report within 24 hours and take action if necessary.
How do I post a free ad?
Click the "Post Free Ad" button in the top navigation. Choose a category, fill in your item details, upload photos, and publish. It's completely free for standard listings!
How do I edit or delete my listing?
Go to your profile and find the listing under "My Ads". Click the edit icon to modify details, or use the delete option to remove it.
What are promoted ads?
Promoted ads give your listing extra visibility. You can choose packages like "Featured" or "Top Ad" that highlight your listing in search results. Promoted ads typically get 5-10x more views.
Is it safe to buy on Mudah.my?
We take safety seriously. Always meet in public places, inspect items before paying, and use our in-app chat. Never transfer money before seeing the item.
